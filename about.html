Hi, I'm Rebeca Mousser

Information Systems student passionate about making everyday tasks easier and more efficient.

My Strengths

I’m naturally detail-oriented and organized, which helps me spot inefficiencies and create clean, simple systems for solving them. I enjoy breaking large problems into smaller steps and making workflows easier to understand and follow.

  • Strong problem-solving and logical thinking
  • Reliable, consistent, and focused on follow-through
  • Comfortable explaining technical ideas clearly
  • Good at creating structure out of complexity

What Motivates Me

I’m motivated by helping others feel more confident and in control of their work. When a process becomes simpler and smoother—saving people time or stress—that’s incredibly rewarding to me. I love the challenge of learning new skills and seeing how technology can be used in practical, meaningful ways.

Every semester in the Information Systems program pushes me to think more critically about how systems affect real people, and how thoughtful design can make a huge difference.

What I’m Passionate About

I’m passionate about using technology to make life easier, not more complicated. I love tools, layouts, and workflows that feel intuitive and calming. Anytime I can turn something messy into something clean, organized, and user-friendly, that’s when I’m at my best.

The Kind of Work I Want to Do

I want to work in roles where I can bridge the gap between people and the systems they use. Whether improving business processes, organizing data, or simplifying tools, my goal is to help teams work smarter, not harder.

  • Improving and documenting everyday business processes
  • Using data to make tasks clearer and more efficient
  • Helping teams adopt better tools and workflows
  • Creating structure and clarity where things feel overwhelming

Long term, I hope to be someone others rely on when they need clarity, calmness, and efficiency in their work.
